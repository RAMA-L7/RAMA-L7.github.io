✨ Magical Prompt Formula

Structure your prompt like this:

  • Role: Who the AI should act as
  • Objective: What outcome you want
  • Instructions: How the AI should behave or format
  • Data: What content or context to use
You are a resume reviewer. Help me improve this resume line-by-line. Here's the current version: [paste resume]

🧠 R.A.C.E. Prompting

  • Role: You are a productivity coach
  • Action: Create a daily schedule
  • Context: For someone learning VLSI
  • Example: Include task breakdowns

🥪 Sandwich Prompt Method

This method wraps your prompt in a structure that stabilizes output:

Start by stating your role as an expert. Analyze the input. End with a clarifying question or summary.

🔁 Chain-of-Thought + Self-Check

This structure boosts reasoning and output reliability:

Think step-by-step. List your assumptions. Solve the problem. Then double-check your logic.

🔒 Rama’s Prompt Design Rules

  • 🎯 Always define your goal before writing
  • 🧩 Use structure: role, instruction, constraints, data
  • 💬 Add examples to guide tone and length
  • 🔄 Iterate by tweaking and testing
  • 🧠 Reflect: ask the AI to critique itself
